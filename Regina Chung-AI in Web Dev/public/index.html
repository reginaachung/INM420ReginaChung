About Me

I'm an interactive designer with a fine arts background, which gives me both empathy and a unique sense of aesthetics. I've always been passionate about pushing my work further. Starting from the spark of an idea, putting my full energy into it, and seeing it come to life is what gives me the most fulfillment.

To me, design isn't just about making things easy to use. It's about solving problems and adding elements that can heal, bring joy, and even feel essential in everyday life. My goal is to blend my artistic roots with design thinking to create work that's emotional, practical, and people-centered.
